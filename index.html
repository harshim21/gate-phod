GATE

The Graduate Aptitude Test for Engineering(GATE) is a very prestigious exam amongst Engineering and Science Graduates and is conducted every year by one of the IITs amongst the Nine Indian Institute of Technologies namely IIT Bombay, IIT Delhi, IIT Kharagpur, IIT Kanpur, IIT Guwahati, IIT Roorkee, IIT Madras, IIT Bangalore, or the IISc Bangalore. GATE is a door to opportunities for candidates seeking admission to the Masters in Technology (MTech), Master of Engineering (ME), Ph.D., or jobs in PSUs. GATE 2024 will be conducted for a total of 29 papers. It will be a Computer-based Test (CBT), i.e., in online mode only.
